HOW TO PLAY KNIGHTS ATTACK

Controls:

Use joystick 1 to move your knight.

Press FIRE on joystick 1 to swing your sword.

Hold FIRE for 2 seconds to start/restart a game.

Objective:

Kill enemy knights to get points.

Avoid enemy knights when they are swinging their swords.

Enemy knights will turn red right before they attack.

Avoid arrows that fly across the screen.
