HOW TO PLAY PINBALL

Controls:

Press FIRE on joystick 1 to launch a ball.

Press LEFT on joystick 1 to flip the left flipper.

Press RIGHT on joystick 1 to flip the right flipper.

Objective:

Use the flippers to keep the ball in the air.

Hit the bumpers to get points.

Red bumpers give quadruple points.

You have 4 balls, when they are gone it's game over.
